Here is how to play

  • You have three minutes to find as many words as you can
  • Only words from the dictionary are allowed. No proper names, abbreviations, or contractions.
  • The letters must be adjoining in a 'chain'.
    (Letter cubes in the chain may be adjacent horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.)
  • Words must contain at least three letters.
  • No letter cube may be used more than once within a single word.

Scoring is as follows

  • Fewer than 3 Letters: no score
  • 3 Letters: 1 point
  • 4 Letters: 1 point
  • 5 Letters: 2 points
  • 6 Letters: 3 points
  • 7 Letters: 4 points
  • 8 or More Letters: 11 points
